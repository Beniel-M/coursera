Chicken




Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world. Owing to the relative ease and low cost of in comparison to animals such as cattle or hogs, chickens have become prevalent throughout the world.

Beef




Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Humans have been eating beef since prehistoric times. Beef is a source of high-quality protein and nutrients.

Sushi




Sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients, such as seafood, often raw, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.
